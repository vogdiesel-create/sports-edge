## The Big Decisions Made This Week

### 1. K-Props: Permanently Dead

51W-50L final. The last 8 bets were ALL losses. The model systematically overestimates strikeout counts by 1-2 Ks. This isn't fixable without a fundamental rebuild (SwStr% model, Statcast data, etc.).

**What we learned:** K/9-based K prediction hits a ceiling around 55% accuracy on OVER/UNDER markets. The vig eats any marginal edge. To make K-props work, you need something like Podhorzer's xK% (R2=0.892 vs our ~0.70).

### 2. RBI + Strikeouts Markets: Disabled

OT RBI: 11W-28L (28.2%), -$956. OT Ks: 12W-13L (48%), -$279. Both DISABLED Apr 30. These were pure leaks.

### 3. TB Juice Cap at -160: Implemented

|---------|--------|----|-----|
| Within -160 cap | 38W-12L | 76.0% | ~30% |
| Beyond -160 cap | 40W-21L | 65.6% | ~4% |

The cap was the single best decision of the project's history. It cuts out heavy-juice bets where edge gets eaten by vig, AND it resolves the EV dead zone problem (stale lines cluster in heavy-juice territory).

### 4. HA Line Filter: u4.5 Only
