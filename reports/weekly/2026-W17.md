## The Big Finding: GAME MODEL OVERS ARE THE ALPHA

| Side | Record | PnL | WR | CLV |
|------|--------|-----|----|-----|
| **MLB OVER** | **5W-0L** | **+$642** | **100%** | **+0.44%** |
| MLB UNDER | 10W-12L-1P | -$170 | 45% | -0.49% |

This is the bombshell. Game model UNDERs, which we thought were the edge, are actually losing money. OVERs are perfect. The CLV data explains it: our OVER picks beat the closing line (positive CLV), while our UNDER picks consistently buy worse than closing (negative CLV).

**What this means:** The model finds genuine edges on overs where the market underprices run-scoring potential. On unders, we're likely chasing the same plays as everyone else and getting worse prices.

We unlocked OVERs on Apr 25 (EXP-005). Both picks won immediately. Sample is tiny (5 bets) but the direction is unmistakable.

**Recommendation: Disable game model UNDERs or raise UNDER threshold to 10%+ edge. Focus game model on OVERs.**
