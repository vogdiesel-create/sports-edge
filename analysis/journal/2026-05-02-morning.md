### The "Stale Line" Hypothesis (H040 - NEW)

The 15-20% EV band likely captures **stale lines in transition**:
- OddsTrader scrapes at a specific time
- Books are actively moving these lines
- By game time, the "edge" has evaporated
- CLV data supports this: only 37% positive CLV overall

Meanwhile, 25%+ EV bets are **true mispricings** that persist to game time (14-0 record).
And 10-15% EV bets are **structural inefficiencies** too small for books to bother correcting.

### Does the Juice Cap Solve This?

YES, partially. The -160 cap blocks 56% of dead zone bets (19/34).
- Dead zone beyond cap: 10W-9L, -18.0% ROI (blocked, good)
- Dead zone within cap: 9W-6L, -0.0% ROI (break-even, tolerable)

**Conclusion: No additional filter needed.** The juice cap already eliminates the bleeding. Within-cap dead zone bets are flat, not destructive.
